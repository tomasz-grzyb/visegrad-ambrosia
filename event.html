About the Seminar

The project will culminate in an open final seminar that brings together all project partners, their institutional colleagues, and interested students and doctoral candidates. This event will feature presentations of the complete project findings, providing a holistic view of ragweed's mycobiome and metabolite profiles across Central Europe.

We will supplement research presentations with thematic lectures expanding knowledge about ragweed invasiveness and its regional impact. The seminar will create a forum for discussing potential applications of our findings for future ragweed management strategies.

By bringing together participants from various disciplines and sectors, we aim to translate our fundamental research into actionable knowledge for stakeholders dealing with ragweed invasion. The seminar will also serve as a platform for exploring future research directions and potential follow-up projects, building on our established international cooperation framework.
